Alumni and Parents

For the olds.

Connect with Club Running


Recently graduated and already missing your Club Running pals? Parent of a Club Runner who wants to stay in the loop about what we're up to? Look no further than this page! The best way to stay up-to-date on general Club happenings is to join the Alumni Listserv , where we send out semesterly recaps, racing/cheering opportunities, and other information. We only send out 3-4 emails/semester on this list, so you never have to worry about us filling up your inbox (unlike the main mailing list [sometimes]). Please reach out to the Webmaster if you experience any issues joining the list.

Check out what our members are up to right now on the Club Instagram and YouTube!


Donate to Club Running


We also accept donations! Your contributions help Club Running meet its annual operational expenses. Donations allow us to:

  • Subsidize members' social/racing dues

  • Organize great meets at venues like Lannigan Field and Panorama Farms

  • Hold fundraising events that benefit the broader community

We are deeply grateful for any support you provide. Donations can be made via Venmo. To donate by any other means, please reach out to our treasurer.
